What We Do

A mission to solve a problem

From the frontlines, I report to you that this must become the reality for millions of children around the world. And it can be done. I know firsthand. Extended family networks are exhausted, even destroyed. Foster homes are often perfect and wonderful—but will always be too few. If we are to offer this basic right to as many children as we possibly can, we simply cannot afford to rule out any one type of care for this terrific number of dear hearts, sweet faces, and precious individuals Working together, we must make a difference. We can make a difference. And, moreover, I absolutely maintain: it really is so easy.


Below are some of the essentials these dear hearts, sweet faces, innocent souls and precious individuals need to survive.

Education

We are trying to put up some buildings to educate the young generation and if we get more Donars we can work hand in hand to provide more infrastructures





Medical

To sensitize the youth both at school and at home about Hiv/Aids through music, dance and drama and about positive living.

Build up Medical facilities/infrastructures and with your Donations, We can make a differance


Healthy Food

Dar-Us-Salaam Charity Organization provides food, clothes, soap and other staffs and we also teach them about agriculture to be able to provide them selves with food





Pure Water

Dar-Us-Salaam Charity Organization provides pure water, fruit juice and other staffs and we also teach them about typhoid and others so that they learn to protect them selves from such diseases




Developmental Issues

We are trying to create awareness on developmental issues eg poultry, capentry, crafts, bricklaying, cultural activities, savings, environmental protection etc.


Agriculture

Access improved exotic animals to the vulnerable people






Shelter

Provide a home for street children and any other marginalized vulnerable groups of the community
